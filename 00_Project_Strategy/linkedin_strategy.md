# Project Cammelot — LinkedIn Applied Research Content Strategy

## Brand Identity

**You are**: An Applied Research Expert who doesn't just talk about AI — you run
live experiments in a simulated society and share the data.

**Visual Brand**: 16-bit SNES RPG screenshots from Cammelot. Immediately 
recognizable between all standard AI screenshots and corporate dashboards.

**Tone**: Data-first, accessible, provocative. Always backed by simulation data.

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. "Applied Research Report" Series
Weekly simulation results with hard numbers.

**Examples:**
- "Applied Research Report #1: In Cammelot, mortality risk increased by 12% 
  purely from GP administrative interruptions."
- "Applied Research Report #2: After 1,000 cycles, the bias in our AI triage
  system accumulated to a 40% higher delay rate for seniors."
- "Applied Research Report #3: Eliminating 90% of admin freed 680 consultation
  slots — but hospitals increased volume instead of quality."

### 2. "Security Audit" Series
Red Team attacks on the Agentic Mesh.

**Examples:**
- "Security GAP Alert: How a forged Agent Card corrupted the healthcare logistics
  of 5,000 people in Cammelot."
- "I introduced a malicious agent into the A2A mesh. It took 3 ticks to access
  every patient record in the town."

### 3. "The IST vs SOLL" Comparison
Side-by-side simulation runs showing the impact of AI transformation.

**Examples:**
- "IST: 48 deaths/year in Cammelot. SOLL (AI-native): 33. Here's the data."
- "The €2.8B IZA transformation budget runs out July 1, 2025. Here's what
  Cammelot looks like the day after."

### 4. "Sector Expansion" Teasers
Preview of Cammelot Education, Retail, Research modules.

**Examples:**
- "What happens when retail agents in Cammelot start pushing healthy food based
  on patient FHIR data? Is it prevention or a privacy leak?"
- "In Cammelot High School, AI tutors freed 30% of teacher time — but only 40%
  of students kept pace with personalized paths."

## Research Questions Queue

### Healthcare (Priority)
1. The Admin Paradox: Does automating 90% of admin improve care quality, or do
   hospitals just increase volume?
2. A2A Deadlocks: What happens when GP and Specialist have incompatible FHIR
   schemas? How many ghostings result from protocol failure?
3. Digital Twin Triage: Can proactive heart failure prediction reduce ER pressure
   by 30%+?
4. The Treeknorm Cascade: If one specialty violates the norm, does it domino to
   others?

### Bias & Ethics
5. Long-term Bias Accumulation: At what cycle count does senior mortality
   diverge significantly from baseline?
6. Echo Chambers: Do agents reinforce each other's biases when they discuss
   patient "therapy compliance" via A2A?
7. The Demographic Lottery: Is your healthcare outcome in Cammelot determined
   by your spawn parameters?

### Security
8. Agent Card Forgery: How quickly can a malicious agent compromise the mesh?
9. FHIR Data Exfiltration: Can any agent read any patient's records?
10. Message Injection: Can fake "treatment complete" messages cause patients to
    stop seeking care?

### Cross-Sector
11. Retail-Health Cross-over: Ethical implications of commerce agents using
    medical data.
12. Education Burnout: Does AI tutoring actually reduce teacher admin, or does
    it create new admin?

## Engagement Tactics

1. **Interactive Polls**: "I'm running 1,000 cycles tonight. Should I invest in
   'Meer Tijd voor de Patiënt' (€3.23/q) or an 'AI-Triage Agent'? Vote now,
   results tomorrow."

2. **Open Research Questions**: "What should I test next in Cammelot? Drop your
   scenario in the comments."

3. **The Shadow Run**: Reference the 24/7 running simulation on your website.
   "While you were sleeping, 12 agents ghosted in Cammelot. Here's why."

4. **Sector Expansion Voting**: "Next Cammelot module: Education or Retail?
   The winner gets built live next week."
